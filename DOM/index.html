Iron Man

About

Iron Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer Stan Lee, developed by Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby, he first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 (March 1963). Iron Man is one of Marvel's most iconic superheroes and a founding member of the Avengers.

Creation & Development

Iron Man was created by Marvel Comics in 1963. Tony Stark is a genius inventor, billionaire industrialist, and philanthropist who builds a powerful armored suit after being captured by enemies. The armor keeps him alive and grants him superhuman strength, flight, advanced weapons, and artificial intelligence support. Over the decades, Iron Man has become one of Marvel's most popular superheroes through comics, animated series, movies, and video games.
